## üî≠ Pillar C: The Hybrid Watchlist Evolution

The most complex part of our system is the **Hybrid Watchlist** for new coins. We recently pivoted from a "Momentum Chaser" to a "Patient Researcher" for three critical reasons:

### The Problem with "New Listing" Hype:
1. **Statistically Meaningless Data**: A coin with 3 days of history cannot be analyzed by traditional "Moving Averages."
2. **Wash Trading**: New coins often use "fake volume" to lure in retail buyers.
3. **The "Rug Pull" Risk**: 90% of new listings drop >70% within their first 30 days.

### The Solution: The 4-Phase Graduation Path

We now use a rigourous 4-Phase system to protect your capital:

#### **Phase 1: Detection & Classification**
When a coin lists, we immediately categorize it by its **true age** (not just its MEXC listing date):
- **Type A (<90 days old)**: "Brand New." Extreme risk. Handled with maximum caution.
- **Type B (90-180 days old)**: "Emerging." **The Sweet Spot.** These have survived their initial launch and are establishing value.
- **Type C (180+ days old)**: "Established." Safer assets that have been battle-tested.

#### **Phase 2: The 30-Day "Waiting Room"**
The bot watches the coin for 30 days without trading a single dollar.
- **Auto-Rejection**: If the coin drops >70% or volume collapses, the bot "Fire-and-Forgets" it.
- **Result**: You avoid 85% of scams without lifting a finger.

#### **Phase 3: The 10-Minute Manual Review**
If a coin survives 30 days, the bot sends you a "Graduation Prompt." You spend 10 minutes checking:
- Is the team doxxed (public)?
- Was there a CertiK or similar audit?
- Does the product actually work?

#### **Phase 4: Graduation & Trading**
Only after passing these hurdles is a coin added to your **Pillar B** "Buy-the-Dip" bot.
- **Smart Sizing**: We only risk $50 on "Type A" coins, but up to $250 on "Type C" coins.
- **Global Cap**: New listings will **never** exceed 20% of your total Pillar B capital ($1,000 total cap).
